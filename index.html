Ph.D PANIPURI

Pani Paru is a refreshing twist on traditional Indian street food, combining crispy puris with flavorful, spiced water. It delivers the perfect balance of tangy, spicy, and sweet flavors, bursting in your mouth with every bite. Loved by people of all ages, this snack is a must-have at parties, street stalls, and festive gatherings. With its crunchy shell and zesty pani, it creates an explosion of taste and texture in every serving. Whether you call it Pani Paru, Golgappa, or Puchka, one thing is certain—it’s absolutely irresistible!
